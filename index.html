Phil Smithson

Chief Executive Officer

As CEO, Phil is responsible for making On-Off Group the top innovation agency in the Philippines. Today, the company employs some of the most experienced Human-Centered Design practitioners in the country. On-Off Group has helped leading Filipino and global organizations use Design Thinking and User Experience Design to develop their most innovative products, services, and programs. Phil’s ultimate goal is to encourage Filipino business leaders to employ empathy to address their customers’ and stakeholders’ needs so that products and services made through the lens of Human-Centered Design becomes the standard in the country.

Raymund Villanueva

Head of Business for QR Ecosystems

Raymund holds an MBA degree from University of San Francisco and has worked for fortune 500 companies leading marketing and analytics organizations within Yahoo and the Gap. His experience in these companies focused on utilizing big data, marketing technology, and creativity in delivering truly innovative and hyper personalized digital experiences to grow global brands. In 2013, Raymund moved back to the Philippines in hopes to further digital within the region. Raymund is currently the Head of Business for QR Ecosystems in PayMaya. He is responsible for expanding cashless payments in the Philippines. He continues to make an effort to be well-versed in the digital space as the digital landscape evolves, aiming to put the Philippines on the global digital map, one innovation, one brand at a time. Raymund also holds a Management Engineering degree from Ateneo and is an expert digital marketer with deep expertise in SEM, SEO, email, display, mobile, video and social media. He is co-founder of a top Performance Content Marketing Agency, Blankslate Creative Inc. and an instructor teaching digital marketing and social media part time.

Jay Dementillo

Lead UX / Visual Designer

With 10+ years of experience working in New York City to San Francisco and now Singapore, Jay Demetillo has almost seen it all in the design world. Jay has worked with Twitter, Pinterest, Yahoo, and now Grab. Jay was an adjunct professor at CCA in San Francisco and has taught/spoken in China for ACG. He's also worked on notable projects such as the Bay Lights, Gaspar Brasserie, San Francisco's Park System SFPark. Dan Matutina

Co-founder, Creative

Dan is represented by illustration agencies Agent Pekka and Vision Track. His style is described as a combination of angular, graphic shapes layered with hand-painted textures. Dan’s illustrations have appeared in print, digital and animation. He has worked on projects for brands such as Apple, Google, Microsoft, Pinterest, Airbnb, Samsung, WIRED Magazine, Fast Co., Wallpaper*, The Guardian, The Wall Street Journal, Coca-Cola, Havaianas, Heineken, Uniqlo to name a few. Dan has also worked with studios and agencies such as Pentagram, Frog Design, CP+B, Wieden+Kennedy, Droga5, Leo Burnett NY, Ogilvy & Mather NY and Tool of NA. At Plus63 Design Co., he develops graphic design identities and systems for brands. The studio experiments with new ideas, extending its scope to include prototyping furniture pieces or custom objects for memorable retail experiences, developing business strategies for brands, designing solutions for nonprofits, and taking on extensive projects such as rebranding a university's athletic program. He has lectured at the University of the Philippines - College of Fine Arts.

Alexis Collado

Senior Product Designer

Alexis leads design at Kalibrr (YC W13), a Silicon Valley company helping millions of South East Asians find better opportunities by transforming the way jobseekers find jobs and companies hire talent. FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

I've submitted my RSVP form but I want to edit my responses! What do I? >

You may answer the RSVP form again (please put the same email you used before!), but take note that we will only count your registration from your most recent submission! So please re-check all the other talks and workshops you still wish to attend!

Tell me more about the Talks >

Aside from the info stated in the Program Schedule, the UX&Chill Talks will also be live streamed from the comfort of your own homes! You may join the Zoom call itself or you may opt to watch the live stream instead through the User Experience Society Facebook page: https://www.facebook.com/UXSoc To be able to join the zoom call, you must register at least a day before the talk you want to attend!

Tell me more about the Workshops >

The UX&Chill Workshops introduce a wide array of topics relating to UX Design and Development taught by some of the most esteemed professionals of the User Experience community. This year, we are also introducing beginner and intermediate workshops for some select topics - UI Design & Creating your own Portfolios - that are sure to harness and test your skills as an aspiring UX professional. To secure your slot in these workshops, you must register at least a day before the workshop you want to attend!

Will the workshop also be live streamed? >

The workshops will NOT be live streamed as it will only be accessible to those that have registered. A recording of the workshop will be sent to each participant's email a few days after the event.

How many slots are there for each talk and workshop? >

As we are limited by our Zoom plan there will only be 90 slots available for EACH talk and workshop, so don't miss your chance and register now!

Are walk-ins allowed? >

Walk-ins are allowed depending on how many sign-ups there are! We will open walk-ins the day before or on the day itself so if you weren't able to join watch out!

Is the event open to the public? >

The event will be open to the public, but priority slots will be given to our partner organizations and ADMU students.

Where will the event be held? >

The zoom link for the event will be sent to you via email once you have registered for the event.

How many days is the event? >

UX&Chill will be a 5-day event starting on November 30 until December 5 (Except on Wednesday)

Is there an entrance fee? >

For this year's event, UX&Chill will be free & open to anyone who registers!

Will the event be recorded? >

Yes, each event will be recorded and uploaded in our YouTube channel "UX Society PH" in order for you to watch again just in case you encounter any connection issues
